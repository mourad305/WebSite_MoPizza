About us

We are the best Pizza ever

In 2017 Mourad Ghaly came to Des Moines from Egypt. After a few years working as a laborer, he decided to do what he had done in his native Egypt. "Bake Bread". The best and only way, he said, was to bake it in a brick oven. So along with a few of his friends he built his 20' by 20' brick oven.

In 2020, still having the brick oven, he decides to start make Italian pizza in Des Moines county. In March 2020, Mourad Start to look for place for rent in the active mall. In fact, Mourad found good place in Jordan Creek Mall.

Now he is very popular because of the sauce made on the pizza


Sauce Story

When Mourad was in Egypt, he was working with an old man, and he learned the secret ingredient of the sauce that he is now popular for.

Why our pizza is the best

We bake pizza dough manually in the old way and do not use baking improvers. We use a refractory brick oven and we use natural wood to heat the oven and do not use a gas oven, in addition to the secret sauce that has been distinguished for many years.

Types of Secret Sauce

  • Red Seret Sauce
  • White Secret Sauce
  • Blue Cheese Secret Sauce
